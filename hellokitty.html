Biography

  • Full name: Kitty White
  • Birthday: November 1st

  • Hobbies: Baking cookies, collecting cute items (Hello Kitty Wiki)

Five Fun Facts

  2. She weighs 3 apples and is 5 apples tall
  3. Her love interest is a cat called Dear Daniel
  4. Hello Kitty is the ambassador for Japenese Tourism in China and Hong Kong, where she is most popular
  5. She was first made in 1974
